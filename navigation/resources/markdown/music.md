# Music & Audio Resources

## Collections

- **CitizenDJ**: Free-to-use audio and video collections from the Library of Congress
  - https://citizen-dj.labs.loc.gov/

- **Free Music Archive**: All royalty free
  - https://freemusicarchive.org/

- **FreeSound**: Free sounds
  - https://freesound.org/

- **Mod Archive**: World's largest collection of music modules
  - https://modarchive.org/index.php

- **Ringtone Banger Archive**: Google Drive with ringtones for download
  - https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1gmdJvP_TZ9NlcV3av16Vvbp1vvXbBcuc

- **SoundBible**: Free Sound effects
  - https://soundbible.com/

- **Soviet Music**: Archive of Soviet Music
  - http://sovmusic.ru/

- **Synth Archive**: Synth archive with guides
  - https://teenage.engineering/guides

- **UbuWeb**: Sound and Artists
  - https://www.ubu.com/sound/index.html

- **Video Game Music**: Demoscene and video game music streaming radio
  - https://mw.rat.bz/davgmsrl/

- **WavSource**: Free Wav Files and sound files
  - http://wavsource.com

- **YouTube Royalty Free**: YouTube Free Music
  - https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCht8qITGkBvXKsR1Byln-wA
