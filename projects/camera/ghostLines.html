GHOSTLINES DETECTOR

Ghostlines Motion Detector

Visualize motion in your webcam/camera feed! Display these cool, customizable, vector fields straight from your input source!

Customizations:

- Detection: Fine-tune by adjusting the sensitivity and vector scale
- Webcam Filter: Choose between colour and grayscale displays
- Motion Colour: Customize vector colors (red, green, or grey)

Status: Inactive
Center Movement: (0, 0)
Change Points: 0
Display Mode: Color
Camera: Front (Mirrored)

Methodology:
Tracks the center of mass movement and draws vectors from areas of change toward the direction of motion, displaying trails of movement.
