About the Collection

#pokemoncards

My physical -> digital pokedex :)
I still don't fully understand how my collection has grown to be this size.. My papa introduced me to the cards when I was around 8 years old with a stack of japanese pokemon cards, and since then I have just been taking in cards whenever I come across them. In 2023, I decided to organize the cards by pokedex number, and this is the collection you see here today. Honestly, as of now only 2/5 of my binders are displayed here, but fun fact: I have never purchased a pack of pokemon cards myself. the entire collection has accumulated from friends and strangers over the years. I have accrued first editions, shadowless, and other rarities.
Anyways, please enjoy my digital archive of my physical Pokémon card collection.

* There are no duplicates on the front facing cards :)
